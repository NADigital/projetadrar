The famous sportsnewspaper L´Equipe published today an interview with Nikola, writting about a possible transfer from Kiel to Montpellier. Niko wants to put some things straight. "In contrast to what was published, I didn´t say that I would not remain in Kiel, I said that I will not extend my contract there. It is not the same. I never hide the contacts I have. All of them are interesting. There are Barcelona, Ciudad, Montpellier. I know that Montpellier makes very big efforts to make me come back and I am not insensible. You understand clearly why. It is the club of my heart. My parents live there and my brother plays for the first team. So, yes, I say it, Montpellier has my preference, but nothing is made, nothing is signed in this moment."
